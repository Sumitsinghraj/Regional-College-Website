Infrastructure

Campus & Facilities

Regional College spreads over 7 acres of vast and sprawling Campus. The campus is in the Sitapura institutional area in the outskirts at Jaipur City. The area is a hub for education and research. The college campus has been designed and constructed under the supervision of highly qualified architects and consists of lecture rooms, tutorial rooms, computer labs, conference hall, laboratories, workshop, library, and administrative office, boys hostel, play fields etc. with lush green lawns and pollution free environment. The campus is fully wired and is safe place and the environment is highly conducive for academic learning. It helps the student to prepare itself for the next 30 years of the career in government, industry or business.

Lecture Rooms & Conferences

Well equipped ‘V’ shaped lecture theatres for better vision of the students. College promotes seminars for the exposure to the students. There is an open air theatre of the capacity of 2000 people for the cultural and academic presentations. All lecture hall are fitted with audio/visual equipments. College plans to connect all the class rooms with internet connection for online lecture support.

Residence & Food

Regional College has well furnished separate hostel for boys & girls to accommodate 100 boys in the campus and 60 girls near the college. the college offers an air cooled hostel rooms with the double/triple sharing basis, hostel offers the warmth, security and full care. It has a clean environment and nutritious food. The airy and spacious rooms are the apparitional for the values of mutual understanding, collective living, sharing of joy and sorrow. The independence and identity do every inmate is respected by the residents. Around the clock warden helps to maintain the discipline. All the rooms are wired and have internet facilities. Students can ask for a terminal or can get his own laptop for the net connection in the hostel. You will feel 24 hours globally connected. Common facilities like: TV, Telephone, Refrigerators, Games, Mess, necessary furniture in the rooms are taken care of.

Canteen

A well maintained canteen with all modern facilities serving lunch and other eatables for the day-boarders is kept under the charge of a senior faculty member who ensures hygienic quality of the food that is served in the canteen.

Drinking Water

College takes full care to provide hygiene in as much possible ways where drinking water also came in one of the priorities. Entire Campus is supplied with R O purified drinking water

Guest House

Hotel Meenakshi Palace, one of the many establishments of Ruchira properties, is our official guest house for non-Jaipur residents. For Regional College guests, it provides 50% discount on the tariff rates.
